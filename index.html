Chapter II  ·  The House

From Cherry Hill
to Springfield,
one shoe at a time.

For years our shop lived at Cherry Hill Mall in New Jersey, where we became known for something specific: treating luxury footwear with the care it deserves, at prices that made sense. Designer houses repair shoes once; we have done it a thousand times.

We are bringing the same bench, the same tools, and the same standards to Springfield Mall in Northern Virginia. Drop something off on your way past Nordstrom Rack. Pick it up looking new.
