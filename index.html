I

Chapter I  ·  The Services

What we do, done by hand.

Forty years at the same bench. Shoe repair is the craft — women's high heels are the daily bread. We treat a twelve-hundred-dollar pump the way we treat a forty-dollar loafer: carefully, and only with what it needs.

  1. I

    Women's High Heels — Top-Lifts & Heel Tips

    The most-asked job at our bench. Worn-down top-lifts replaced, stiletto tips re-tipped, scuffed heel covers re-wrapped. Most pairs in fifteen minutes while you shop the mall — pumps, stilettos, kitten heels, block heels, boots.

    Specialty
  2. II

    Christian Louboutin Red-Sole Restoration

    Factory-matched lacquer on the iconic red sole, buffed by hand and sealed to last. Pumps, slingbacks, booties — your reds leave looking new.

  3. III

    Resoling & Reheeling

    Full soles and half soles in rubber or leather. Heel rebuilds for women's pumps and men's dress shoes. Boot heels, lifts, taps — done in-house.

  4. IV

    Leather Repair, Recoloring & Stretching

    Scuffs, scratches, faded uppers. Custom stretching for width or length, orthopedic adjustments. We return color and suppleness to leather that still has miles left.

  5. V

    Handbags, Luggage & Jackets

    Handle and strap replacement, lining and zipper repair, leather-jacket restoration. If we can't fix it, we'll tell you honestly.

  6. VI

    Alterations, Keys & Shine — New in Virginia

    Hems, waist and sleeve adjustments, zipper replacement, tuxedos and occasion dresses. Keys cut while you wait, hand shines, laces, cleaning.

Forty years
at the bench,

For forty years — first at Cherry Hill Mall in New Jersey, now at Springfield Mall in Virginia — we have done one thing: repair shoes by hand. Mostly women's heels — top-lifts, tips, full reheeling on pumps and stilettos and the Louboutins that come in for their reds. Plus resoling, leather, alterations. Designer houses repair shoes once; we have done it tens of thousands of times.
